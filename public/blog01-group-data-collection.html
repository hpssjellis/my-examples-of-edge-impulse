High School Group Data Collection of Acceleration Data using the students cell phones and a Nano 33 Ble Sense Arduino

  1. Load EdgeImpulse.com I like to load both the forum and Doc into their own tab before login in.
  2. In Edge Impulse pull down your login name and select an Impulse or make a new one.


  3. Now start uploading data by either connecting your Arduino. See instructions or much faster for acceleration, sounds or images use your cell phone. You could also use your laptop or desktop computer for the Sounds or images but for Images the cell phone is much easie to position the camera.
  4. Get used to the "Dashboard" lots, but not everything is here.
